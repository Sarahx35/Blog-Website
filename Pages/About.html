About Me

Born and raised in Eastbourne, I'm a seaside girl at heart.

If you happen to pop down to the South East Coast, I have written a blog post on a bakery called To The Rise, which has risen to fame through their delicious cinnamon buns.

As you will have gathered from the title of this website, my main passions lie in baking and make-up. Over the years, I have to come focus my time more on the food side of things. So, in this blog you will most likely see predominantly food content.

Being blessed with a sweet tooth, means that I spend most of my free time discovery the latest bakeries and cafes and seeing what new bakes they have to offer. This is mainly what I write about in my blog posts.

It is a dream of mine to open my own bakery, but until that day arrives, I will continue to enjoy visiting other local delicacies.
